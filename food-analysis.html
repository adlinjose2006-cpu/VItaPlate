💡 Nutrition Tips

  • Proteins: Build and repair muscles. Aim for 0.8-1g per kg body weight daily
  • Carbohydrates: Your body's main energy source. Choose whole grains
  • Fats: Important for hormones and health. Prefer healthy unsaturated fats
  • Calories: Average daily intake: 1800-2500 calories (varies by activity)
  • Fiber: Helps digestion. Aim for 25-30g daily from vegetables and grains
